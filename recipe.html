RECIPE NAME: Avocado Sushi Rolls

INGREDIENTS:

  • Avocado
  • Rice
  • Seaweed sheets

HOW TO PREPARE:

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and cook according to the instructions on the packaging.
  2. Place a sheet of seaweed on a bamboo sushi mat.
  3. Spread a thin layer of cooked rice evenly on top of the seaweed, leaving about 1 inch of the seaweed uncovered.
  4. Place avocado slices on top of the rice, covering the entire surface.
  5. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, applying gentle pressure to keep it compact.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Once all the rolls are made, use a sharp knife to slice them into bite-sized pieces.
